Cheeseburger

Description

This is a classic American cheeseburger. Cooked the way of the backyard grill. A summer staple in many American homes. There are many varieties and toppings that one can add, but this recipe keeps it simple with common ingredients. Feel free to mix it up how you like though!

Ingredients

  • 1/4Lb of 80/20 Ground Beef
  • 1 Hamburger Bun
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tomato
  • 3 slices of pickles
  • Spinach
  • 2 slices American cheese
  • Mayo
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Form the ground beef into a patty, a little bit larger than the size of the bun. It will shrink during the grilling process.
  2. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Preheat grill on high
  4. Once grill is nice and hot, put the burger patty down on the grill and let sit for about 4 minutes.
  5. Once you can lift the burger without it sticking, flip the burger and place the slices of cheese on top. Cook another 4 minutes.
  6. Once the burger is done, take off the grill. You can toast your buns if you would like.
  7. Spread mayo, ketchup and mustard to the top of your top burger bun.
  8. On the buttom burger bun, place the lettuce, then the burger patty.
  9. On top of the burger patty, place two sliced of tomato and 3 slices of onion and the 3 pickle slices.
  10. Enjoy your burger.
