Pizza Recipe

Description

This pizza recipe is for a New York Style pepperoni pizza. Thin, but chewy crust, with melty cheese and a sweet sauce that combines to make the perfect at home pizza.

Ingredients

Dough

  • 500gr Bread Flour
  • 350gr warm water
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1 Tbsp EVOO
  • 1 Tbsp Sea Salt

Sauce

  • 1 16oz can of San Marzano Peeled Tomatoes
  • 2 medium cloves of garlic - minced
  • 2 sprigs fresh basil leaf
  • 1 Tbsp EVOO

Steps

Dough

  1. Mix the flour, sugar, salt and yeast.
  2. make sure water is at around 105 or 110 degrees then add it to the dry ingredients.
  3. Mix the ingredients for a minute, then add the EVOO.
  4. Mix until all bits are just wet, then let sit for 30 minutes and then cover with a wet rag or saran wrap.
  5. Contune mixing dough until dough passes the Window pane test.
  6. Once dough passes the window pane test, form the dough into a ball. Oil the insides of a container with EVOO and put the ball of dough inside the container.
  7. Let the dough ball rest in the fridge for 72 hours.

Sauce

  1. Pour the contents of the canned tomatoes into a large bowl.
  2. After washing hands, crush the tomatoes by hand, until the tomatoes are somewhat chunky, but not smooth.
  3. Add the minced garlic.
  4. Add the EVOO.

Putting it all together

  1. Insert pizza stone and preheat oven to 550deg 1 1/2 hours before planning to cook pizza.
  2. Shred mozzarella cheese
  3. flatten the dough out by hand into a circular shape.
  4. Add your sauce in the center and push it outwards in a spiraling motion./li>
  5. Top the pizza with cheese.
  6. Add any other desired toppings.
  7. Slide pizza onto stone and cook for ~10 minutes, turning once halfway through.
  8. Once pizza is done, remove from oven and place on a raised wire rack to let sit for 3-5 minutes before cutting.
