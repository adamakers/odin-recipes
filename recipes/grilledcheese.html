Grilled Cheese

Description

This grilled cheese is simple to make, but a classic and sure to fill the stomach. It uses few, but common ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of preferred bread.
  • 3 packets of American Cheese
  • Softened butter

Steps

  1. Warm a pan on the stovetop.
  2. Butter a side of the bread and place on top of the heated pan.
  3. Add the cheese to the top of the bread. Make an even layer. You may have to tear the cheese to get an even coating.
  4. Add the second piece of bread on top of the cheese and butter the top.
  5. Check the bottom of the bread to see when it is golden brown. Once its crispy and golden, flip the sandwich.
  6. Cook again until golden brown. Once cheese is melted and bread is toasted, remove from the pan.
  7. Let rest for a minute or two then cut diagonally and enjoy.
